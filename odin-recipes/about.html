About Us

At Olives and Thyme, we have been serving our community since 1990. Our founder, Christos Sykas, started the business with a vision to provide high-quality, home-style meals to his customers. With his passion for cooking and a strong work ethic, Christos built a loyal following and the restaurant soon became a neighborhood favorite.


Today, our team of chefs and staff continue to carry on Christos's legacy by using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients in our dishes. We believe that good food starts with good ingredients, and that's why we source our ingredients from local farms and suppliers whenever possible. We take pride in the quality of our food and the service we provide to our customers, and we look forward to serving you soon!
