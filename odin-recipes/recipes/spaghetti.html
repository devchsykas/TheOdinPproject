Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 x 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese, to serve

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, until the onion is soft and translucent.
  3. Add the minced beef to the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned all over and cooked through.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, sugar, and a pinch of salt and black pepper to the pan. Stir everything together and bring to a simmer. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer gently for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it has thickened slightly.
  5. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it in a colander and return it to the pot. Pour the bolognese sauce over the spaghetti and stir everything together until the spaghetti is evenly coated in the sauce.
  6. Serve the spaghetti bolognese in bowls, with freshly grated Parmesan cheese sprinkled over the top.
