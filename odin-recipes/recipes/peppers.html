Stuffed Peppers Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers, any color
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Chopped fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C.
  2. Cut off the tops of the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes from inside. Place the peppers upright in a baking dish.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sautÃ© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking up any large chunks with a spatula.
  5. Add the salt, black pepper, paprika, oregano, and basil to the skillet and stir to combine.
  6. Add the cooked rice and diced tomatoes to the skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Spoon the beef mixture into the bell peppers, filling them about 3/4 of the way full.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil from the baking dish, sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top of the stuffed peppers, and return to the oven.
  10. Bake uncovered for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  11. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley and serve hot.
