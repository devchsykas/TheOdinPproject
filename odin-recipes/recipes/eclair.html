Classic French Eclairs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped

  3. Add the flour to the saucepan and stir vigorously with a wooden spoon until the mixture forms a ball and pulls away from the sides of the pan.
  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and let the dough cool for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the eggs, one at a time, stirring vigorously after each addition until the dough is smooth.
